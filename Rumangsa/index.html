Muhammad Hafiz Waliyuddin was born on July 12, 1994 and studied at SMA Negeri 1 Mempawah, then earned a Bachelor of Informatics Engineering and Master of Electrical Engineering from Tanjungpura University. He started his career by founding startup Angkuts, a digital-based cleaning service that was halted due to the pandemic. In 2021, he opened Coffeeshop Rumangsa, a coffee shop that offers a quiet and authentic atmosphere, prioritizing taste, soft acoustics and tranquility. The name Rumangsa, from Javanese, means “taste” or “self-awareness.” In 2025, Rumangsa expanded to Singkawang City, still carrying the same philosophy: serving tranquility in a cup of coffee.
